Why Create a Video Resume?

  • Show, don't tell: Demonstrates your communication, clarity, and confidence.

  • Showcase soft skills: Especially important for client-facing or team-based roles.

  • Boost recall: Recruiters remember faces and voices more than plain PDFs.

  • Global edge: Particularly useful when applying for remote or international opportunities.
